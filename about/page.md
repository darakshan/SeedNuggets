# About Seed Nuggets

Seed Nuggets is a collection of surprising ideas, elaborated in several ways.  It is inspired by Christopher Alexander's classic "A Pattern Language", which was written well before the advent of internet access and hyperlinks, but clearly foresees that in its organization.

Some day these nuggets might be organized linearly and turned into a book, or a movie, or a series of articles, or who knows what.  Time will tell.
